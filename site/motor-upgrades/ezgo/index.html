FAQ

EZGO motor upgrade questions.

What's the top speed of a EZGO cart with the White Lightning motor?

Customers regularly hit 25–28 mph on a EZGO with our high-output motor, stock controller, and healthy 48V batteries. Add a Navitas controller and lithium pack and 30+ mph is realistic.

Which EZGO models do you support?

We build motors for: TXT, RXV, Marathon, Medalist, Express. If your year or trim isn't listed, call Charlie at 832-832-1993 — five minutes on the phone and you'll know.

Will this work with my EZGO stock controller?

Yes. The motor is designed to drop in and run on the factory controller. You'll see a major top-speed and torque bump immediately. Pair it with a Navitas DC controller later when you want full programmability.

How fast can I get it back?

4–7 business days for standard turnaround once your motor lands at the shop. Rush options available.
