FAQ

Questions we get every day on the phone.

Will this work with my stock controller?

Yes. Our high-output motor is engineered to bolt straight in and run on your existing stock controller. You'll see a big jump in speed and torque without buying anything else. When you're ready for more — Navitas controller, lithium pack, full AC kit — we can build that path with you.

How fast will my cart go?

Most customers see 25–28 mph on the motor swap alone with healthy batteries. We've seen 30+ mph on builds with upgraded controllers or lithium packs. Travis Holm went from 14.8 to over 27 mph on tired batteries — that's our floor, not our ceiling.

What carts do you support?

EZGO (TXT, RXV, Marathon, Medalist), Club Car (DS, Precedent, Onward, Tempo), Yamaha (G-series, Drive, Drive 2), Star Cart, ICON, and Evolution. If you're not sure, call Charlie at 832-832-1993 — five minutes on the phone and you'll know.

How long does turnaround take?

Standard mail-in motor upgrades go out within 4–7 business days of arrival. Rush options are available if you need it back faster — just ask when you call.

What's the warranty?

One year on every motor we ship, from the day it arrives at your door. If something fails inside that window, we make it right.

Yes. Polk County Golf Carts, Tejas Golf Carts, and other shops resell our work. Call to get on dealer pricing and bulk motor orders.
